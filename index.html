-- Meet Niket --

Welcome to the world of Niket Gupta, where fashion meets artistry in a seamless blend of modeling and photography. With a passion for both the runway and behind the lens, he is a multifaceted talent poised to captivate audiences and redefine the boundaries of beauty and creativity.

Niket Gupta epitomizes grace, sophistication, and style. With an innate ability to embody the essence of any designer's vision, he brings garments to life with elegance and flair. From haute couture to streetwear, Niket Gupta effortlessly commands attention, captivating audiences with his magnetic presence and impeccable poise.
